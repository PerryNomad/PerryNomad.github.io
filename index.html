✔ Who am I?

• I specialize in user experience(UX) and user interface(UI) design. I'm here to improve your business processes, increase audience engagement, and customer loyalty through user-centered UX/UI design.

• Besides UX/UI design, I have a lot of experience in front-end development, which gives me a huge advantage and understanding of how things works.

• There is a problem behind every project, and you need the best solution for your business. Hiring a UX/UI designer should be an investment, not an expense. You need a specialist eager to provide value and aiming to deliver more than you asked.

If you're looking for such designer for your next project, just message me and I will do my best!

✔ What is good UX design is?

• Good UX design creates a positive experience for your users by anticipating and fulfilling their needs.

✔ What is good UI design is?

• Good UI design makes it possible to present the interface as convenient, clear, logical, not overloaded and easy to perceive for users.

If you're looking for the best UX and UI design for your next project, lets' talk!

✔ Process

• Every designer has their way of doing things. My process is simple and agile. I start with user needs and merge them with the needs of your business.

• Economy and simplicity are the keys to creating great UIs. My iterative approach embodies this idea to produce measurable results that continually evolve to fit your business goals and user needs.

• You want to improve the UX/UI design of your product

✔ FAQ

Q. Why your portfolio doesn't show all your work.

A. I respect the rules of the companies I worked for, and I have a valid NDA contract that does not allow me to disclose projects.

Q. How many clients at a time do you work with?

A. I take on a limited number of projects. This quality control means I can give each one exactly what it needs —­ focus and time.

Q. Can you offer any guarantees?

A. Customer satisfaction is my priority, and I always go the extra mile. I'll be working until you're thrilled with the result.

Q. What are your primary tools?

A. Figma, Photoshop, Invision, Zeplin, etc.

Q. Can you create illustrations?

A. No. My area of expertise is UX and UI design. I'm not an illustrator.
